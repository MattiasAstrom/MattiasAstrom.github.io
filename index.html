    About

    Hey there, I'm Mattias, a passionate game programmer currently studying at The Game Assembly in Stockholm. As a dedicated game developer, I'm always seeking new opportunities to grow and improve my skills.

    Like many others my love for games came during early childhood with games such as Snake, Super Mario and Ratchet & Clank. My professional journey in the world of game programming started with extensive research during my three-year education at Södertörns Högskola, where I dove deep into analyzing different aspects in games such as mechanics and their impact on the player experience. I explored the intricacies of UI/UX design, information distribution, the role of randomness in game mechanics and other subjects such as game marketing.

    After gaining a strong theoretical foundation in game design, I enrolled in a two-year game programming education at The Game Assembly, where I honed my practical skills in programming theory, C++, 3D math, Directx11 graphics, OOP, data structures, design patterns, game algorithms, AI, project management, and agile development using Scrum.

    Through eight diverse projects, including Unity/Unreal 2D/3D games and in-house engine development, I gained hands-on experience in game programming, developing my skills in languages, algorithms, graphics, AI, and game design.

    Moving forward, I'm actively seeking an internship opportunity for the fall of 2023 to further enhance my skills and contribute to exciting game projects. I'm deeply interested in exploring and improving game mechanics to create an immersive player experience, with a passion for creating fun and challenging games that captivate players and leave them wanting more.
